CityZen - A City-Guide App Case Study

Project information

  • Category: UI/UX design
  • Project date: 01 September, 2023

Looking to explore the best a city has to offer? Look no further than CityZen, your ultimate companion for all things adventure. With a plethora of options, CityZen has been thoughtfully designed to cater to individuals across all interests – be it a culture vulture, foodie, history buff, or simply an explorer at heart. From hidden gems to vibrant cafes, charming museums to lively concerts, CityZen has got you covered. Customize your itinerary, save your favorite spots, and get personalized recommendations based on your interests. With CityZen, you'll experience the city like never before!

Problem Statement

For travelers exploring a new city, it can be overwhelming to plan out their itinerary and find the best things to see and do. With limited time and an abundance of options, individuals often struggle with making the most out of their trip and end up missing out on some great experiences.

Solution

CityZen is the ultimate solution for individuals looking to explore the city. With its comprehensive guide to the most beautiful things to do in the city, users can effortlessly discover hidden gems, vibrant cafes, charming museums, and lively concerts all in one place, providing a centralized hub for all their travel needs. With CityZen, achieving an unforgettable experience in any city is now easier than ever before.

Design Process

Colour Palette

The colour palette reflects the dynamic atmosphere of the bustling city with a vibrant Cool and Warm mix of colors. Taking inspiration from the city sealink, I've incorporated its iconic stamp seamlessly into the logo design, creating a unique and eye-catching image that captures the city's essence. The colour scheme is a true representation of the city's liveliness and spirit, sure to leave a lasting impression on all who see it.

Wireframes

UI Designs

Login Pages
  • A login feature in the app gives user a personalised experience.
  • The logo here keeps the continuity of the colour scheme followed in the app.

Home Page
  • Exclusive Deals Hub: Access to an exclusive deals section, showcasing the latest and hottest deals from Cityzen's partnerships with local businesses.
  • Real-Time Trending Updates: Stay in the loop with Cityzen's real-time updates on trending events and activities. Be it a spontaneous concert, a new restaurant opening, or a special event, the app's dynamic feed keeps users informed about the latest and most popular happenings in the city.

Explore and Social Page
  • Wide Array of Categories: Cityzen stands out with its extensive range of categories, ensuring there's something for everyone. From diverse culinary delights and thrilling events to relaxing weekend getaways, users can effortlessly explore and discover the city's offerings in a variety of categories. Whether you're a foodie, culture enthusiast, or adventure seeker, Cityzen has you covered.
  • Socialize Section for Shared Experiences: This section allows users to share their city adventures with others. Post about your outings, share photos, and read about other users' experiences and reviews.

Profile
  • Personalized Event Calendar: Cityzen's profile section offers a personalized event calendar where users can seamlessly add their favorite destinations and events. This feature ensures that users can plan and organize their city exploration efficiently, keeping track of activities that interest them.
  • Saved Destinations Folder: The Saved Destinations folder is a user-friendly feature where individuals can curate and save their favorite places for quick access.
