Fabric Exquisite - Shopping Website

Project information

  • Category: UI/UX design
  • Project date: 22 June, 2022

Fabric Exquisite is a stylish clothing brand that celebrates the beauty and elegance of all things fabric. Their new website design reflects this philosophy, blending elegant and minimalistic design elements to create a captivating online experience for their customers. Fabric Exquisite's website uses minimalistic design with muted colours and stunning photography to showcase their clothing collection. With curated content, clean pages and intuitive navigation, the result is a captivating online destination that perfectly captures the essence of the brand.

Problem Statement

The shopping store sales are declining due to its outdated website design, which is failing to create an impression on online customers. With competitors introducing elegant and user-friendly websites, Fabric Exquisite is a brand which is losing out on customers who are looking for a more captivating online shopping experience.

Solution

To address Fabric Exquisite's declining sales, a new website design has been developed that uses a minimalistic and elegant approach, which appeals to the brand's target audience. The new design integrates stunning photography, curated editorial content, and an intuitive navigation system to provide customers with an effortless and sophisticated online shopping experience. By implementing this new design approach, Fabric Exquisite will be able to attract and retain customers, and stand out from its competitors.

Design Process

Colour Palette

Soft Neutrals: Incorporate gentle shades of whites, off-whites, beige and light grays to establish a clean and uncluttered visual environment. These soft neutrals evoke simplicity and provide a neutral backdrop for product displays.

UI Designs

Home Page
  • Sleek and Minimal Home Page: Emphasize the home page's sleek and minimal design, boasting a carefully curated color scheme that enhances visual appeal and user engagement.
  • The user interface is intuitively designed for easy navigation, ensuring a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience right from the start.
  • The logo of Fabric Exquisite keeps the continuity of the colour scheme followed in the app.

Categories
  • Clean and User-Centric Categories Page: The minimal color scheme ensures clarity, and the user satisfaction is prioritized through easy navigation and a visually appealing layout. Users can effortlessly explore and find products within their preferred categories.

Product Page

  • Visually Pleasing Product Presentation: The product pages feature a visually pleasing design that focuses on highlighting the details of each item. The minimal color scheme ensures that the products take center stage, creating an immersive experience for users to explore and make informed purchase decisions.

  • Recommended for you Section: A section showcasing related or complementary products beneath the main product details. This feature encourages users to explore additional items, potentially increasing the average order value and overall engagement with the website.

  • Intuitive Call-to-Action Buttons: Clear and intuitive call-to-action buttons for actions such as "Add to Cart." These buttons are strategically placed and designed for easy access, contributing to a seamless and user-friendly interface.

Cart and Checkout

  • Effortless Cart Management: The intuitive interface streamlines the process of adding products to the cart, creating a hassle-free journey from selection to checkout.
  • Seamless Checkout Experience: Users can efficiently review their selected items, make adjustments, and complete the purchase with ease. The design prioritizes clarity and simplicity, contributing to a positive and efficient checkout process.
