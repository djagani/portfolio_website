FashFit - A Virtual Clothing Try-on System

Project information

  • Category: UI/UX design
  • Project date: 10 November, 2022

FashFit is the virtual clothing try-on solution that is a perfect fit for anyone seeking an immersive shopping experience. Utilizing leading-edge Augmented Reality and Machine Learning technologies, FashFit makes it effortless to try on various clothing items from the comfort of your home. With precision joint detection and clothing item recognition, FashFit puts you in virtual fitting rooms where you can effortlessly assess the style and fit of your desired clothing.

Problem Statement

One of the biggest challenges faced by individuals who shop for clothing online is the inability to try on the clothing and assess whether it suits their style and physique.

Solution

FashFit provides a virtual clothing try-on system that solves the challenge of assessing clothing suitability without visiting a store. By harnessing modern Augmented Reality and Machine Learning technologies, FashFit enables individuals to test clothes online before making purchase decisions, thus saving them time and reducing uncertainty.

Design Process

Colour Palette

Elegantly Warm Palette: The palette features shade of scarlet, brown, and beige tones, creating an elegantly warm ambiance. Scarlet adds a touch of bold sophistication, brown brings a grounded and earthy feel, while beige maintains an overall soothing and minimalist aesthetic. This combination exudes warmth and richness, elevating the shopping experience with a sense of refined simplicity.

UI Designs

Login Pages

Minimalist Login Page: The app has a minimalist login page, designed for simplicity and user-friendliness. The minimal color scheme contributes to a clean and easy-to-understand UI, ensuring that users can quickly and securely access the app without unnecessary complexities.

Home Page
  • Daily Offers on Home Page: The home page features a section for daily offers, providing users with exclusive deals and discounts. This not only enhances user satisfaction by offering cost-effective choices but also encourages frequent engagement with the app to discover new and exciting offers.
  • Seasonal Outfits Recommendations: The app is capable to recommend seasonal outfits based on trends and user preferences. This personalized touch adds value to the user experience, helping them stay fashionable and in line with the current season.

AR Try-On
  • Real-Time Clothing Simulation:The real-time aspect of the AR feature, where users can see how clothing items look on them instantly. This immersive and dynamic experience adds a layer of excitement to the virtual try-on process.
  • Virtual Fit Adjustments:The app allows users to make virtual adjustments to their outfits. Whether it's adjusting the colour of a dress or the size of a pair of pants, users can customize the virtual try-on experience to suit their preferences.

Profile
  • Aesthetic Profile Page: Showcase the profile page as a visually pleasing and aesthetic space. Users can easily view their selected items, manage their preferences, and enjoy a seamless add-to-cart process. The aesthetic design contributes to an enjoyable and personalized shopping experience.
  • User-Centric Cart Management: Demonstrate the app's user-centric cart management system on the profile page. Users can easily add selected items to their cart, review their choices, and proceed with a smooth checkout process. This feature ensures convenience and efficiency in the user's shopping journey.
